Task and Purpose of the Expert System (Auntie Midwife)

Auntie Midwife, an expert system designed to facilitate and ease the stress of receiving medical help
and advice during pregnancy. It seeks to reduce the stress of standing in long queues, which has proven
to be both energy and time-consuming. With proper maintenance, the chatbot could offer 24/7 availability,
meaning it would be accessible at any given time. This means the system can be void of any closing time
or break time, which is a problem that most current medical systems have.


The chatbot would be efficient in terms of finance and time. The pregnant lady could save on
the cost of transportation and other fees that would be paid at the hospital.
Then again, in recent times where people are advised to stay home because of the imminent spread of illnesses,
there are fewer people-to-people interactions. This would be helpful in reducing the spread of the diseases.


Impacts

This chatbox would help pregnant women around the world keep updated during pregnancy on how to stay , and also reduce
healthcare professionals' workload by reducing hospital visits, reducing unnecessary treatments and procedures,
and decreasing hospital admissions and readmissions as treatment compliance and knowledge about their symptoms improve.



Knowledge Design & Engineering

The knowledge base is focused on user message response during the initiation of conversation.
It should be natural to respond with a suitable back-end logic. The idea behind this is to provide the necessary information to pregnant women.
